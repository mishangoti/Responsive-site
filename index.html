Veg Mayo Grilled Sandwich

Recipe by Mishan Goti



Servings

2 servings


Prep Time

10 Minutes

This sandwich may look pretty but it is also insanely healthy! As it has plenty of vegitables. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  1. Finely chopped onion (half cup)
  2. Finely chopped capsicum (half cup)
  3. Finely chopped cabbage (half cup)
  4. Finely chopped carrot (half cup)
  5. Finely chopped green chillies (two tablespoon)
  6. Finely chopped coriander (two tablespoon)
  7. Mayonise (3 tablespoon)
  8. Solt (according to test)
  9. Black papper powder (according to test)
  10. butter
  11. Bread

Utensils

  1. One Bowl
  2. Knife
  3. Cutting Board
  4. Grill Machine

Directions

  1. Take one bowl and mix all six vegitables.
  2. Add Mayonnaise to the mixture
  3. Add solt and black papper (according to your test)
  4. Mix the mixture properly
  5. Take brades, do buttering on inner side of bread for two pieces.
  6. Now we will do stuffing on the bread and on the top of it put other piece of bread to make sandwich.
  7. Now we will put sandwich in grill machine, but before that do buttering both sides of the sandwich.
  8. After 6 to 8 minutes it will be ready.
  9. Now it is ready to serve in breakfast or as afternoon snack.
  10. Serve sandwich with tomato ketchup.
